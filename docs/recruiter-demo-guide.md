# Recruiter Demo Guide

## Product Positioning

BetCopilot AI should feel like a modern AI decision product, not a raw pipeline inspector. The experience now emphasizes elegant operator-facing cards first and technical traceability second.

## Best Demo Sequence

1. Open the demo UI on desktop or mobile width.
2. Show the premium hero and segmented input selector.
3. Use the sample prompt:
   `LeBron over 27.5 points and Lakers moneyline tonight`
4. Click **Analyze**.
5. Walk through:
   - extraction summary
   - bet candidate cards
   - live odds cards
   - QA and trust panel
   - concise explanation
6. Open **View technical details** to show trace depth and structured JSON.
7. Run **Eval demo** and show the card-based quality metrics.

## What Recruiters and Hiring Managers Should Notice

- the interface feels productized and mobile-friendly
- results are human-readable before technical inspection
- ambiguity, confidence, and QA are made explicit
- technical rigor still exists through the trace drawer and eval panel
- the repository demonstrates both AI pipeline engineering and product judgment
