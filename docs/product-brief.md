# Product Brief

BetCopilot AI is an enterprise-grade applied AI system that transforms unstructured betting chatter into validated, structured decision support. The product focus is betting intelligence, not wager execution: extract the bet, validate the context, compare it to market reality, and present confidence with operational traceability.

## Primary User Value

- Convert noisy inputs into consistent structured bet objects
- Surface ambiguity early instead of hiding it inside a generated paragraph
- Enrich extracted picks with live market context
- Show confidence, QA status, and trace metadata in one view

## Supported Inputs

- chat text
- article text
- social posts
- URLs
- screenshots and bet slips (phase 2 scaffold)

## Outputs

- structured bet objects
- confidence scores
- ambiguity flags
- event, team, player, and market extraction
- live odds enrichment
- QA results
- eval metrics
- latency, cost, and reliability trace
